The Solution

Smart Attribution Without Smartphone

One master account. Unique QR per vendor. Automatic attribution.

Unique QR Per Vendor

Generate vendor-specific QR codes with embedded IDs. All payments route to your single UPI account.

Auto Attribution

Notification listener reads UPI credits and automatically logs transactions to correct vendor. Zero manual work.

Daily Settlement

Real-time dashboard shows each vendor's earnings. One-tap cash settlement with exportable reports.

Financial Identity

Build verifiable transaction history for vendors — opening doors to formal credit in the future.

How It Works

4 Simple Steps to Financial Inclusion

1

Create Vendor Profile

QR owner adds each vendor with name and location. App generates unique QR code with embedded vendor ID.

2

Print & Display QR

Each vendor displays their printed QR at their stall. Customers scan on any UPI app — PhonePe, GPay, Paytm, BHIM.

3

Auto Attribution

UsPe reads incoming UPI notification, extracts vendor ID, and automatically logs transaction. SMS fallback included.

4

Daily Settlement

Dashboard shows each vendor's total. Pay out in cash. Export daily reports via WhatsApp.

About UsPe

Born from Real Market Observation

"

While purchasing goods in a local market in Ajmer, a vendor gestured towards a shop across the road and said 'Uspe kardo' ('Do it on that one'). The payment went to someone else's QR code. There was zero record of which vendor had received what amount. That single real-world moment became the foundation of UsPe.
